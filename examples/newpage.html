Olivia Rodrigo

Rodrigo released her Sour album on May 21, 2021, which consists of 11 songs. When first released, her number 1 song out of the album was "Drivers License". Coming soon, on September 8th, 2023 she will be releasing her new album, Guts. She recently released two singles called "Vampire" and "Bad Idea Right" to give a little taste of her new album.
